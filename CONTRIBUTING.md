# Contributing

Thank you for helping make this list better! 🎉

## How to Suggest a Repo

1. [Open an issue](../../issues/new) with the title: `Suggest repo: owner/repo-name`
2. Include:
   - Repository link
   - Why it belongs in the finance/fintech/quant category
   - Approximate star count

## How to Report a Bug

Open an issue with the title: `Bug: [brief description]`

## Guidelines

- Only suggest **finance, fintech, quant, crypto, or trading-related** repos
- Repo must be **public** and have at least **100 stars**
- No self-promotion spam

## Automated List

The weekly Top 10 is fully automated — repos are ranked by the number of new stars gained
in the previous 7 days. Suggestions go into the tracked list and will appear if they grow fast enough.
